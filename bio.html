SHORT BIO

Born in Lima - Peru and currently based in New York, I'm a researcher and multimedia artist working with technology, storytelling and visual experiences. I am interested in computer graphics, 2D and 3D development and immersive experiences.

Back home I worked in documentary and advertising as an Assistant Director & Videographer before moving to NYC to pursue a Master's degree at NYU's Interactive Telecommunications Program (ITP).

Upon graduation I have created AR and VR experiences, and presented projects at Microsoft and Viacom. Currently, I am a Post-Doc Resident Fellow at New York University.
