NE

Director, creative coder and art director.

NE is a musical Virtual Reality Experience created for the
Viacom NEXT Fellowship in collaboration with the NYC Media Lab.
The experience brings the user into it's own personal audio-visual
electronic party. The project was showcased at Viacom Headquarters
(NYC - 2017), and at the Rio2C Creative Conference (Rio de Janeiro - 2018).


Tools: Unity + C# + HTC Vive + Oculus Rift.
