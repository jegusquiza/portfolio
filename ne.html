ABOUT:

The idea behind this project was to use the capabilities of Virtual Reality to produce an immersive experience centered on a piece of music from Viacom's music library. This project was created for the Viacom VR Fellowship in collaboration with NYC Media Lab.

Approach: I decided to create an experience that would immerse the user into their own personal electronic music venue. The song that I chose, was an instrumental eletronic piece, that started with a slow pace, nd progressively evolving and finally arriving at a climax. I've always been a fan of music, lasers and the dance between them; so I created a choreagraphy of lasers that would evolve and dance acording to the music.
