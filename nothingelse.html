ABOUT:

Art cover for Andrea Mendoza's new release "Nothing Else". A song about gratutide and appreciation for the simple and valuable things in life.

Approach: In terms of the image, the singer was imagining a starry night in the mountains, with wind and a sense of being in a magical place. An important element in the image were the curtains, giving the sense of movement and wind.
