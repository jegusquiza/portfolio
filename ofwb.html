Our Future With Books

In Collaboration with: Alejandro Matamala, Cristobal Valenzuela, Kenzo Nakammura, Daniella Garcia and Scott Reitherman.

"Our Future with Books" is a mixed reality application for the Hololens that
optimizes our experience of learning from printed books. This project was part of the
2017 Microsoft Design Expo. The original challenge was: Intentional Design for Positive Cultural
Impact in Mixed Reality.


Tools: Mixed Reality - Speculative - Design
